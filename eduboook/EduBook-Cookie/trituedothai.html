Jewish Wisdom is an ambitious mind book on improving self-study, memorization and analysis - the things that have made the Jews stand out, occupying important positions in the media, banking and innovation awards around the world.

Jewish Wisdomdoes not stop at the confines of a philosophy or skill book.
